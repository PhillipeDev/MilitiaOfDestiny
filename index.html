About Us
Mainly an adult clan with a little childish humor running through the middle.

Most of us are located in Europe with some of us in the US. Regardless of your timezone, you can always find someone on if you need help.

Important to us is that you try to socialize with our members and admins. We are very aware that some people are simply shy or introverts as we have some members who are that way, we only ask that you try to socialize and do not keep to yourself. Destiny is a community afterall and we will not make you feel unwelcome.

Now, all you have to do is survive the initiation ritual... When one chooses to walk the way of the Mandalore, he is both hunter and prey.
